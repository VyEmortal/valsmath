Problem 6 — 1.4 Exercises (Determining Continuity)

Determine whether \(\sin^{-1}(x)\) is continuous at \(x = 2\).

SOLUTION The domain of the function \(\sin^{-1}(x)\) is \([-1, 1]\). Because \(x = 2\) is not included in the domain, \(\sin^{-1}(x)\) is undefined and therefore discontinuous there.
